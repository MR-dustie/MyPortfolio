About Me

Hello, this is Fei!

I am full-time student studying Game Design and Animation in Hong Kong Baptist University. Since 2016, I have dedicated serious time in improving my craft. This website is an accumulation of my sizable work.

More about my background

Throughout my childhood, the consistent highlight of my day was turning on the TV to watch silly goofy cartoons. My elder sister and I would bound over drawing on cheap sketchbooks to create worlds to share with one another. The positives of art had encouraged me to engage in art communities, take art classes, and choose visual art in secondary school.
